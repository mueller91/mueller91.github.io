Deepfake Total

A website to explore and evaluate voice recordings by many state-of-the-art deepfake detection models

Deepfake-total.com is a website where you can test state-of-the-art antispoofing models for human voice. Given a Youtube Link, or an uploaded audio file, several state-of-the-art models return a score between 0 (authentic) and 100 (fake).

The website "Deepfake-Total.com", developed and maintained by me at Fraunhofer AISEC.
